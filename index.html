About Me

After switching my major four times freshman year, I finally stumbled into a UX design class and everything clicked. I’d spent years believing I needed a “practical,” high‑paying career to be successful, but I’ve learned that real success comes from passion and feeling fulfilled. I’ve always loved the intersection of art and technology, and looking back, I’m not sure why it took me so long to pursue the path that feels the most like home.

I’m excited to build a career in UX design, especially within startups, branding, or any space where I can help create something new. My long‑term goal is to become a senior UX designer at a meaningful company, ideally in retail, food, or fashion/beauty. And once I reach that milestone, I hope to take my love of startups even further by opening a franchise in the food industry.

Outside of design, my passions include running, baking, art, reading, and learning. I’m big on self‑improvement, and I’m endlessly inspired by wellness podcasts and journaling. That curiosity fuels my commitment to sustainability and healthy living, values I hope to champion in my future work.
